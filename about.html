Why did I set up Poppins Inspiration?

 

A couple of months ago I began helping my mother to declutter and organise her home. My Mum is in her 80's and as you can imagine had accumulated many belongings along the way. Some belongings have a lot of meaning, memories and happiness attached to them however some become commonplace and the reasons for keeping things can get lost.

 

The change that has occurred in my Mum's home has been wonderful. She has experienced a shift in perspective and now questions her own reasoning around keeping things that she no longer uses. She has also identified the things that she values and these now have pride of place where she can enjoy them. She tells me how much she loves seeing the changes and we also really enjoyed the time we spent together while her home was being transformed!

 

I went on to help another family member ....and then one of my good friends. They both were really excited about the changes that were made and within no time they were sending me photos of more decluttering and organising they were doing. I was getting more and more inspired by this. I realised I was getting as much enjoyment from helping people transform their living spaces as they were. I wondered if this could work as a small business.

 

So why not.... I thought I'd give it go... after all I have been such an 'organiser' all my life...as my friends and family know!

 

So welcome to my new little business! Please LIKE and SHARE the page to let people know about the service and please give me a call to have a chat if you want to know more. I would love to hear from you! Many thanks, Sally
